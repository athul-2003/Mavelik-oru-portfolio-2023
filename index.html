About Me

I am King Mahabali,

The legendary and cherished ruler of Kerala. Despite being a demon king, my rule marked the pinnacle of Kerala's history, and my people held me in the highest esteem. In Kerala I am celebrated as a noble and prosperous ruler who turned my kingdom into a heavenly paradise. My legend lives on as an integral part of the annual Onam festival in this beautiful state.

Experience the Magic of Onam

Onam, the festival of colors and joy, is a vibrant celebration of Kerala's rich culture and heritage. Join us in the festivities!

Witness the breathtaking Pookalam decorations, indulge in the mouthwatering Onam Sadya, and immerse yourself in the traditional dance forms like Kathakali.

Onam is not just a festival; it's a time for family reunions and cultural bonding. The 10-day-long celebrations bring people from all walks of life together to celebrate the spirit of unity and harmony.

During this auspicious time, people exchange gifts, visit temples, and create beautiful flower arrangements to welcome the legendary King Mahabali.
